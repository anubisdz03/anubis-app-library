## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please follow responsible disclosure principles:

1. **Do not** open a public GitHub issue for security vulnerabilities.
2. **Do not** publicly disclose the vulnerability until it has been addressed.
3. Contact the maintainer privately through one of the channels below.

### Contact Channels

| Platform | Link |
|----------|------|
| Telegram | [t.me/AnubisDZTech](https://t.me/AnubisDZTech) *(preferred — fastest response)* |
| Facebook | [facebook.com/Anubis.dz.Tech](https://www.facebook.com/Anubis.dz.Tech) |

### What to Include in Your Report

Please provide as much of the following information as possible:

- A clear description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- The URL or file(s) affected.
- Any proof-of-concept code or screenshots (shared privately).
- Your suggested fix, if any.

## Response Process

| Step | Timeframe |
|------|-----------|
| Initial acknowledgement | Within 48 hours |
| Investigation & assessment | Within 7 days |
| Fix or mitigation deployed | As soon as possible, depending on severity |
| Credit to reporter (if desired) | Upon resolution |

## Scope

This security policy applies to the ANUBIS APP LIBRARY website and its source files. It does **not** cover third-party services, external links, or apps listed in the library.

---

## Responsible Disclosure

We appreciate the security research community. Researchers who responsibly disclose vulnerabilities in accordance with this policy will be:

- Credited publicly (with their permission) after the issue is resolved.
- Not subject to legal action for good-faith security research.
